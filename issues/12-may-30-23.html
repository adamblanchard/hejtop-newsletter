With great power comes great sponsorship

It took one person's bet on me to get my first developer job.

It took one person's belief in me to get my first management position.

It took one person's inspiration in me to get my first director role.

And it took a LOT of people supporting me to start my first company.

Whether you can help someone get a job, sponsor their promotion, or even simply believe in them with everything you got... it makes a huge difference for people‚Äôs growth - so let‚Äôs help each other out!

This becomes even more impactful (and a bigger responsibility!) when we're in positions of power, authority and political influence. Don't underestimate how much you have either. If you're in any kind of lead role and any level of representing others to more senior folks, you can make a big difference by even mentioning someone's name.

Old vs new

We're all here to become better leaders, managers and supporters of the people we work with. There's no magical formula or clear cut theory for doing that. But by evaluating our practices often, and considering if they fall into an "old" or "new" binary way of doing things can help us build our continuous improvement muscles.
