There is no standard API for humans

That makes it hard as an engineering manager because:
1. Everyone is different and has ever changing needs.
2. Many new people challenges pop up on the weekly.
3. You have many responsibilities to balance at once.

While you can't run one approach for everyone, build up your own frameworks for tackling certain themes that leave plenty of space for the above needs.
